## Two Types of Progression

### 1. **Level Progression** (Overall Player Level)
- **What it is**: Your overall player level (Level 1, Level 2, Level 3, etc.)
- **How you earn it**: By accumulating **total points** across ALL activities
- **Progression**: Every 100 points = 1 level up
- **What it shows**: Your overall mastery and dedication
- **Display**: Shown in gold "Level Badge" on main menu

## Why Both Systems?

1. **Levels** = Long-term progression across ALL activities
2. **Difficulty** = Individual skill growth in each specific game  
3. **Achievements** = Fun milestones and rewards for special accomplishments

This creates a rich progression system where:
- You always feel progress (levels, stars, badges)
- Each game has unique challenges (difficulty levels)
- Special moments are celebrated (achievements)
- Everything works together to keep you engaged and motivated!
